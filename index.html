Devices

There are so many devices that are used by people everyday. Let us learn about the most common devices.

Device name Definition Visual
Mobile phones Its is a wireless hand-held device that allows us to make/recieve calls, text one another, entertain,take photos, etc. It is compact and easy to carry around.
Laptops A laptop is like a computer which is portable. It is not as portable as a mobile phone. It's size is a size that can perfectly fit on top of a lap. It has better graphics,memory value and abilities when compared to phones.
Desktop Computer It is like a laptop but it is a greater size. It has multiple storage disks and fits on a table. Unlike laptops, it has an external keyboard and it is not a device that is made to be carried around, it is meant to be powerful for important uses like work or any other necessities.
Tablet It is like a laptop. It lies in between a mobile phone and a laptop. It has a bigger screen than a phone but its smaller than a laptop. It has a touch screen interface and its wireless. Some tablets come with an external keyboard but they are not desktop computers.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence, commonly known as AI is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines,especially computer systems or even robots.Specific applications of AI include expert systems,
natural language processing, speech recognition and machine vision.AI can quickly learn new
concepts and adapt to inputs and even perform human-like tasks with robotic bodies. AI is probably the new civilization if humans ever pass. AI is becoming so huge that in some places, There are restuarants and even
hotels that use AI as receptionsits, as cleaners, waiters, and all those jobs. Many people are constructing
AI that can assemble other robots and do multiple,very difficult human tasks.AI is the new ERA.
